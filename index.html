Hui Wang   |   王辉

Hui Wang is an algorithm engineer in Community Science Department of Kuaishou Technology. Before this, he received his master degree in June 2022, at School of Information, Renmin University of China, under the supervision of Wayne Xin Zhao. He received his B.E. degree in Computer Science and Technology from School of Information, Renmin University of China in June 2019.

He has a broad interest in Recommender System, with an emphasis on Sequential Recommendation. You can contact him by email at hui.wang AT ruc.edu.cn.
